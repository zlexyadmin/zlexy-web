How Our Pricing Works

Clear, predictable, and tailored to your content. No surprises—just the logic behind your quote.

The Formula

We price projects using a simple structure that adapts to language and service needs.

Estimate = Base Rate × Language Complexity × Service Type ± Volume Discount + Add-ons + Rush (if any).

  • Base Rate (admin-configurable): typically €0.10–€0.25 per word. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
  • Language Complexity (multiplier): 1.0× / 1.2× / 1.5× based on the language pair. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  • Service Type (multiplier): 1.0×–1.4× depending on the service (e.g., translation, LQA, transcreation). :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Add-ons (flat or %): e.g., +10%, +€50, +€100 for extras such as certified copies, DTP, or glossary creation. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
  • Rush Fees (percentage): +30% / +50% based on delivery timelines. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
  • Volume Discounts (percentage): –5% / –10% / –15% for larger word volumes. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Currency Conversion: Real-time FX rate applied to quote in your preferred currency. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

Quick Examples

Example 1 (Standard): Base €0.12/word × Language 1.2 × Service 1.0 → €0.144/word. With –10% volume discount → ~€0.13/word.

Example 2 (Rush + Add-ons): Base €0.15/word × Language 1.5 × Service 1.2 → €0.27/word; Rush +30% → €0.35/word; Add-on (+€50) if requested.

For per-minute (AV) or per-hour (interpretation/training) work, we use an equivalent base rate for that service and apply the same multipliers.

What Shapes Your Price

  • Language pair complexity & subject-matter domain
  • Service scope (translation, editing, LQA, transcreation)
  • Volume of content & format/engineering effort
  • Turnaround time (standard or expedited)
  • Optional add-ons (certification, glossary, DTP)
